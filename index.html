Music Technologist, Software Engineer, Georgia Tech Grad

Featured Projects

Turn-Taking for Robotic Musicians

My research was developing a new musical turn-taking model for robotic musicians. I studied group improvisation human musicians, and I used by findings to create a probablistic model and genetic algorithm for AI agent improvisation.

Shimi

We reimagined Shimi, a musical companion robot, to serve the deaf and hard of hearing community. I led a team of undergraduate students in their project work. I also developed a network communication protocol for Shimi.

FOREST

FOREST is an NSF funded project aimed at enhancing trust between humans and robots through sound and gesture. My research focused on developing performances for a group of 10 robotic arms. Specifically, I created a piece riffing on John Conway's Game of Life.

Dynamic Guitar Amp

In a group of four, we are created a JUCE amp simulator plugin with tone control and other basic effects in a signal chain. I focused on the system architecture, building effects, and GUI implementation.

HeartBeats - A Music Dating App

HeartBeats is a dating app that matches users based on music taste. Based on user listening history and music preference, we develop matches and joint playlists for an immediate icebreaker.
